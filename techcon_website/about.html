Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our mission is to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and real-world applications by creating an inclusive platform where innovators, thought leaders, and change-makers converge to shape the future

History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small gathering of tech enthusiasts to a globally recognized conference attracting thousands of attendees each year. Over the years, we have hosted renowned speakers, launched groundbreaking products, and fostered countless collaborations that have driven technological advancements worldwide.

Past Speakers

We have been honored to host a diverse lineup of speakers from various sectors of the tech industry. Past speakers include:

  • Rupert Brun

    • Rupert Brun is the founder and CEO of QuantumLeap Technologies, with over 15 years of experience in quantum information science. He holds a PhD in Quantum Physics from MIT and previously led research at CERN's quantum computing division.

  • Deidre Hanford

    • Deidre Hanford serves as CTO at Silicon Dynamics and chairs the IEEE Semiconductor Standards Committee. With 20+ years in chip design, she's been instrumental in advancing nano-scale semiconductor technology and manufacturing processes.

  • Jakub Paschoski

    • Jakub Paschoski is a leading AI researcher at OpenAI, specializing in natural language processing and machine learning. His work focuses on developing advanced algorithms that enhance human-computer interaction and drive innovation in AI applications.
